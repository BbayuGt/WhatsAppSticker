How to use?

  1. Upload an image using the button above.
  2. Wait for the preview to appear.
  3. Long press (or right-click) the image and select "Copy Image".
  4. Open WhatsApp and paste it into any chat.

Tip: Use a modern keyboard like Gboard for the best copy-paste experience.

Why use this?

While you can make stickers directly in WhatsApp, they often add awkward white borders or cut images poorly. This tool formats your image perfectly for the sticker engine, giving you a cleaner, professional look without the hassle.
